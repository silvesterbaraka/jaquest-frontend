Eligibility Criteria

These eligibility conditions apply strictly to international students from developing countries seeking admission to Indian universities.

Nationality & Visa

  • • Applicant must be a non-Indian citizen
  • • Open to African & Asian countries
  • • Valid passport required
  • • Student visa issued after admission

Academic Requirements

  • • Minimum 50%–60% in last qualification
  • • Higher scores improve scholarship chances
  • • English-medium background preferred
  • • Subject relevance required for chosen course

Merit & Performance

  • • 75%–100% waivers for strong academic records
  • • Consistent performance across years preferred
  • • Entrance tests only for selected programs

Additional Conditions

  • • Study gap acceptable with explanation
  • • IELTS not required for most programs
  • • Medical fitness certificate mandatory
