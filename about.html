About JaQuest Scholarships Agency

JaQuest Scholarships Agency is an education-focused coordination and student-support institution committed to ethical guidance, risk reduction, and student welfare in international education pathways.

Institutional Mandate

JaQuest is not a recruitment agency and does not act as a decision-making authority. Our mandate is advisory and coordinative — ensuring informed student engagement, procedural clarity, and responsible institutional alignment.

Student Protection & Coordination Framework

Context Assessment

Academic readiness and expectation alignment.

Risk Awareness

Academic rigor and cultural realities.

Procedural Guidance

Documentation accuracy and timelines.

Ethical Safeguards

Transparent and non-exploitative practice.

Mental Well-Being

Stress awareness and early support.

Institutional Respect

Aligned collaboration with universities.
